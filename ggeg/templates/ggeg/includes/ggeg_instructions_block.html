  • In Part 2 both you and the other 6 members of your group, with whom you interacted in the first part of the experiment, will receive an amount of points equal to 100.
  • You and the other members of your group will have to choose how many points out of 100 points to keep and how many to invest in a group project.
  • The points you invest in the group project will be doubled and equally distributed among the 6 members of your group, excluding you.
  • Similarly, the amount of points invested in the group project by each other group member, for example participant A, will be doubled and distributed equally between you and the other 6 members of your group, excluding subject A.
    • How payoffs are determined
  • The payoffs you earn in the Part 2 will be equal to:
    100 points minus the amount of points you decide to invest in the group project plus plus the amount of points invested in the project by the other members of your group . multiplied by 2 and divided by 4.
  • Example 1
    Suppose that a participant has decided to invest half of the total amount of points in the group project, i.e. 50 points. Suppose further that the other members of his group have invested a total of 300 points in the group project. The payoff for this participant will be:
    100 - 50 + (300 * 2 / 6) = 150
  • Example 2
    Suppose that a participant has decided not to invest any of the total amount of points for the period in the group project, i.e. 0 points. Suppose further that the other members of his group invested a total of 300 points in the project. The payoff for this participant will be:
    100 - 0 + (300 * 2 / 6) = 200
