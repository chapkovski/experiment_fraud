  • The first part of the experiment is over.
  • Now the second part will begin.
  • In the second part, both you and the other 6 members of your group, with whom you interacted in the first part of the experiment, will receive an amount of points equal to 100.
  • In each period both you and the other members of your group will have to choose how many of these 100 points to keep and how many to invest in a common project.
  • The points you invest in the project will be doubled and will then be equally distributed among the 6 members of your group, excluding you.
  • Similarly, the amount of points invested by each member of your group, for example by subject A, will be doubled and then distributed equally between you and the other 6 members of your group, excluding subject A.
