We will now explain in detail what choices you will have to make in the second part of the experiment. The composition of the group is the same as in the first part of the experiment.

The second part consists of 10 periods, in each of which you will have to make your choices based on the same rules.

At the end of the experiment, if Part 2 is selected for the final payout, we will draw one of the 10 periods randomly with equal probability. Your final earnings will be the earnings you earned in the drawn period plus 3 euros for showing up to the experiment.

In each period of Part 2, both you and the other 10 members of your group with whom you interacted in the first part of the experiment will receive an amount of points equal to XXX .

Based on the XXX points awarded in the period, both you and the other members of your group will have to choose how many points to keep and how many to invest in a group project.

The points you invest in the group project will be multiplied by x and equally distributed among the 10 members of your group, except you.

Similarly, the amount of points invested in the group project by each other group member, e.g. participant 5, will be multiplied and distributed equally between you and the other 9 members of your group, excluding the participant.
