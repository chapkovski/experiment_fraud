    Let’s make an example :

    Suppose that in round t the cost of electoral fraud is equal to 20 points and that Candidate A decides to implement electoral fraud while candidate B decides not to implement it. Additionally, suppose that one member of the ALPHA party and two members of the BETA party choose to vote.

    Therefore, the ALPHA party has more votes than the BETA party.

    The member of the ALPHA party who chose to vote earns 105 points while the other three members of the ALPHA party who chose to abstain earns 105 points plus his or her own personal Y-bonus; the two members of the BETA party who chose to vote earn 5 points, while the other three members of the BETA party who chose to abstain earns 5 points plus his or her personal Y-bonus.

    Candidate A will receive 210-20=190 points while candidate B will receive 60 points.

    The bottom of the screen contains a history panel that will be updated to reflect which party got a highest number of votes in each round. At the end of each round you will be also informed about you earnings in that round and in the previous ones.
